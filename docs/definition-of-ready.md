## Definition of Ready (DoR)
User stories must meet these criteria before being accepted into a Sprint Planning session.

A user story is Ready when:
- The story has clear and testable acceptance criteria
- The business value and goal are understood by the team
- Dependencies are identified and no major blockers remain
- The story is estimated and small enough to be completed within one sprint
- Relevant designs, data, or assets are available
- The team understands how to demo or validate completion
